Eugene Kaurov

Motion designer, freelancer

With over 16 years in the industry, my background is built on a university degree in Graphic Design, which naturally evolved into a career in 2D motion and Art Direction.

I love optimizing workflows and looking beyond standard tools. I practice "vibecoding," using AI to create custom After Effects scripts and design utilities. This allows me to solve problems faster and more creatively.

I work remotely (Moscow, GMT+3) with clients worldwide. I am fully set up for international invoicing. I am open to both project-based freelance collaboration and full-time in-house offers if the fit is right.
